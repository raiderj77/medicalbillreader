# medicalbillreader.com ,  SEO Guidelines

## Primary Content Focus
Medical bill explanation, EOB decoding, CPT code lookup, medical billing errors, hospital billing advocacy, how to dispute a medical bill.

## Keyword Strategy
- Target: "what does [code/term] mean on a medical bill", "how to read an EOB", "how to dispute a medical bill"
- Opportunity: specific CPT code explainers (very long-tail, patients search for exact codes)
- Avoid: broad "health insurance" queries (Healthline, NerdWallet dominate)

## Internal Linking
Always link to the main bill reader tool when discussing how to decode bills.

## Meta Title Format
`[Topic] | MedicalBillReader ,  Free Medical Bill Explainer`

## Schema
Use FAQPage schema. Use HowTo schema for dispute guides. Use MedicalWebPage schema.
Include dateModified (billing codes change annually).

## GEO Requirements
- 40–60 word direct answer blocks under every H2
- CPT and billing code definitions are highly citable ,  write clean definitional content
- FAQ sections are key: LLMs are frequently asked about medical billing
