# medicalbillreader.com ,  Style Guide

## Formatting
- Use tables for code lookups (CPT code, description, typical cost range)
- Bold all medical codes and billing terms on first use, define immediately
- Use numbered steps for dispute processes
- Note effective dates: "CPT codes updated annually ,  verify current codes at cms.gov"

## Terminology
- "EOB" = Explanation of Benefits (always spell out on first use)
- "CPT code" = Current Procedural Terminology (always define)
- "balance billing" ,  define clearly; many patients don't know this term
- "coordination of benefits" ,  define when used
- Use "medical bill" and "billing statement" interchangeably; avoid "invoice"

## Disclaimer (Required on Every Article)
*This is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, legal, or medical advice. For complex billing disputes, consult a qualified medical billing advocate or healthcare attorney.*
